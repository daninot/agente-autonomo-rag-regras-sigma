Creation or renaming of a Windows user account whose name contains the '$'
character — a stealth technique that mimics machine accounts (which normally
end with '$') to hide the account from administrators or evade detections
that don't properly parse the suffix.

- Product: windows; log source service: security
- Detection combines TWO alternative selections (OR via 1 of selection_*):
  (a) account creation EventID with SamAccountName containing '$';
  (b) account rename EventID with NewTargetUserName containing '$'.
- EXCLUDE the legitimate HomeGroupUser$ built-in account on the creation
  event — use a filter_main_* block.
- Condition: `1 of selection_* and not 1 of filter_main_*`.
