Generate a SIGMA rule to detect USB device plug/unplug events on a Windows
host — may indicate physical access attacks, data exfiltration via removable
media, or unauthorized peripheral introduction.

Target product: windows; log source service: driver-framework. Requires the
Microsoft-Windows-DriverFrameworks-UserMode/Operational event log enabled —
document this.

Detection matches EventIDs related to driver loading and Plug-and-Play /
power management events triggered when USB devices are connected or
disconnected.
