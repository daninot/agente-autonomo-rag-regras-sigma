Generate a SIGMA rule to detect modification or deletion of Kubernetes Secrets
— an attacker with sufficient RBAC permissions may create, alter, or destroy
secrets to steal credentials, plant malicious values, or cover their tracks.

Target product: kubernetes; log source service: audit.

Detection matches audit events targeting the secrets resource, covering all
mutating API verbs (creation, deletion, and update variants).

References to include:
- https://kubernetes.io/docs/reference/config-api/apiserver-audit.v1/
- https://commandk.dev/blog/guide-to-audit-k8s-secrets-for-compliance/
