Generate a SIGMA rule to detect PowerShell scripts that modify Group Policy
settings on a Windows host — adversaries abuse GPO modifications to disable
security controls (e.g., SmartScreen) or tamper with policy refresh intervals
to delay enforcement.

Target product: windows; log source category: ps_script. Requires PowerShell
Script Block Logging enabled — document this.

Detection combines TWO conditions that BOTH must match (multiple selections
with `all of selection_*`): (a) script touches the Group Policy system
registry path; (b) script manipulates specific GPO-related value names.
