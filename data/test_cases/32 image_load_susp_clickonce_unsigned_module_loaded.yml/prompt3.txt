Generate a SIGMA rule to detect unsigned or expired-signature modules loaded
by a ClickOnce application — attackers abuse ClickOnce trusted execution to
load malicious DLLs while bypassing SmartScreen.

Target product: windows; log source category: image_load.

Detection combines TWO selections with AND (all of selection_*): one matching
the ClickOnce application deployment directory in the loading process image
path, another matching unsigned or expired-signature status on the loaded
module.
