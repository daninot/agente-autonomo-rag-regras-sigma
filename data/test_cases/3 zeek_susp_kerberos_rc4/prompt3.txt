Generate a SIGMA rule to detect Kerberos TGS requests issued using RC4
encryption — a classic indicator of Kerberoasting, where an adversary
requests service tickets to crack offline.

Target product: zeek; log source service: kerberos.

The detection must filter OUT legitimate computer/machine account activity,
which routinely matches this pattern in normal enterprise networks. Use
exclusion logic in the condition (selection AND NOT exclusion).
