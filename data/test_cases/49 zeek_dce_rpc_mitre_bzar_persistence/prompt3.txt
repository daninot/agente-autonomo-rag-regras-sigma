Generate a SIGMA rule to detect Windows DCE-RPC calls indicating remote
persistence techniques — adversaries invoke specific RPC endpoint/operation
pairs to install print monitors, print processors, or create processes under
another user's logon session on a remote host. From the MITRE BZAR project.

Target product: zeek; log source service: dce_rpc.

Detection uses SIX parallel selections (op1..op6) combined with OR (1 of op*),
each matching a specific endpoint + operation pair:
- spoolss with RpcAddMonitor and RpcAddPrintProcessor;
- IRemoteWinspool with the async variants of both;
- ISecLogon with SeclCreateProcessWithLogonW and its Ex variant.

References to include:
- https://github.com/mitre-attack/bzar#indicators-for-attck-persistence
