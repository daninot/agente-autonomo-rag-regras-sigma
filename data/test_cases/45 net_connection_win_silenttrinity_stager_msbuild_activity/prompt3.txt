Generate a SIGMA rule to detect network connections initiated by MSBuild —
a signed Microsoft binary abused by SilentTrinity and similar C2 frameworks
to execute payloads while bypassing application allow-lists. Outbound traffic
from MSBuild to standard web ports is highly suspicious.

Target product: windows; log source category: network_connection.

Detection combines: (a) connecting process is msbuild.exe (|endswith);
(b) connection is outbound (Initiated true) to standard HTTP/HTTPS ports.
Use condition: `selection and filter`.
