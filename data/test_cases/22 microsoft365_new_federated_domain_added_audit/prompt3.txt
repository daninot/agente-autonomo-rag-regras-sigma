Generate a SIGMA rule to detect addition of a new federated domain in
Microsoft 365 — adversaries abuse domain federation to establish persistent
backdoor access via forged SAML tokens, bypassing MFA and password controls.

Target product: m365; log source service: audit.

Detection combines TWO selections with AND (all of selection_*): one matching
the operation field for domain-related activity, another for creation/addition
verbs. Use |contains on both.
