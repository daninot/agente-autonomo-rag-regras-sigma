Generate a SIGMA rule to detect PowerShell execution from alternate (non-default)
hosts — attackers embed the PowerShell runtime in custom executables to bypass
detections focused on powershell.exe. Logged via the HostApplication field in
PowerShell classic event logging.

Target product: windows; log source category: ps_classic_start.

Detection: match HostApplication marker in the Data field, then EXCLUDE known
legitimate hosts in two groups:
- main filters: standard PowerShell binary paths (System32 and SysWOW64, both
  forward- and back-slash variants), the diagnostic host sdiagnhost.exe, and
  bare "powershell" string;
- optional filters: Citrix ConfigSync and Hexnode Agent.

Use condition:
`selection and not 1 of filter_main_* and not 1 of filter_optional_*`.
