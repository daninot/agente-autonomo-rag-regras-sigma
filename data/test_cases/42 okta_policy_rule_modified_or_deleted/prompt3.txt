Generate a SIGMA rule to detect modification or deletion of an Okta policy
rule — attackers with admin access may weaken or remove security policies
(MFA enforcement, sign-on rules, access controls) to facilitate persistent
access.

Target product: okta; log source service: okta.

Detection matches Okta system log eventType values for policy rule update and
delete operations.

References to include:
- https://developer.okta.com/docs/reference/api/system-log/
- https://developer.okta.com/docs/reference/api/event-types/
